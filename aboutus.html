About Us

VSU Auctions is your one-stop platform to buy, bid, and win exclusive items made for and by Virginia State University students. From limited-edition merch to custom creations and school pride gear, we give the Trojan community a new way to connect and showcase spirit.

Built by students, for students, our goal is to create a digital marketplace where creativity meets competition. Whether you're looking to score cool VSU hoodies, student artwork, collectibles, or essentials, we make it fun and fair through live bidding and flash drops.

Join the auction, represent VSU with pride, and discover the coolest campus treasures while supporting your fellow Trojans!
